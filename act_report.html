WeRateDogs Twitter Data¶

Analysis¶

The dataset used is from a twitter account 'WeRateDogs' that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog such as
This is Maya. She's very shy. Rarely leaves her cup. 13/10 would find her an environment to thrive in
The Rating system is such that the denominator is equal to 10 and the numerator could be greater than 10.
From the dataset I found the following fields of data useful for my analysis; Tweet_id, dog name, dog stage, source of tweet, img_num,tweet favourite count and tweet retweet count.

The following are the insights produced:

  1. There are 3 main sources of tweets:
  • Twitter for iPhone
  • Twitter Web Client
  • TweetDeck with 98% of tweets from iphone users.
